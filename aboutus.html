About Us

The days of gathering everyone together in one place to hold a conference are long gone. With Cloud Conference Room, you can hold your conferences online anytime and anywhere there is an Internet connection. Your conference participants can be spread out around the world, yet still gather together for a conference in your Cloud Conference Room.

Cloud Conference Room is a feature rich online application that gives you the ability to hold productive conferences online. It is an interactive and innovative virtual conference room thatÂ  allows your attendees to communicate with each other through voice, webcam and chat in real-time, while viewing and sharing files and presentations.

Cloud Conference Room gives you full control as the moderator of your online conference. You can assign who can attend the conference, who is allowed to upload files and presentations, who can use the whiteboard and who can interact through voice, video and chat. At anytime, you can enable and disable each attendees access and restrictions.

For a small monthly fee, you can hold virtual conferences with just the click of a button. Forget about airfare and hotel rooms. Web conferencing is so much easier and more cost effective.

Cloud Conference Room is a versatile online solution for many business and educational needs. Virtual online conferences are useful for:

  • Sales conferences
  • Training seminars
  • Educational seminar
  • Online classes
  • Collaboration meetings
  • Any other event where you need a group of people to interact and share information

Whether you need to run a small or large conference, Cloud Conference Room has you covered. Invite attendees from anywhere in the world. Your attendees can participate from their home, office, or anywhere else where they have a computer and Internet connection.
