Frequent ask questions

Do I need a credit card to sign up for the trial?

No. We do not collect credit card information when you sign up for a trial account. You can pay via Paypal when you're ready to upgrade your plan.

Do I need to choose a plan now?

No. Sign up for a free trial on any plan, and you can choose a plan later when you're ready to upgrade. In your free trial period, you can track 1 campaign and access all features.

Is there a discount for non-profits?

Yes. We offer our service to no-profits and charities for free! Sign up first, then just email us and we'll set you up straight away as long as you keep making the world a better place!

What happens at the end of my trial?

You can upgrade to a plan if Cloud Conference is right for you. If not, there's no need to cancel anything. You stay on a free account where you can have meeting with up to 2 attendees

Is there a minimum commitment?

Cloud Conferece Room is a monthly service. There is no contract or long term obligation. If you cancel you will not be billed again.

Is there a plan for high volume customers?

Yes. We're happy to develop a plan that fits higher volume customers. Sign up first, then email us and we'll create a custom plan for you.
