Affiliate Program

Want to benefit from telling others about Cloud Conference Room? Then our Affiliate Programme is perfect for you!

For every aticve sign-up, we pay out a one-off payment of:

  • 15% for a monthly subscription
  • 22% for a yearly subscription

If you introduce only 10 people in a month you could earn anywhere from $70 to $400 in one month, or 100 in one month could earn up to $4,000*.

You don't need to sign anything or change your account settings, as long as you have a Cloud Conference account you can instantly become an Affiliate. As long as you agree to the Affiliate Terms and Conditions.

Your unique affiliate code is stored when a user comes to our site from a link you have sent out. This is stored so when they come to upgrade or buy a pro account we know who sent them to us. This code links directly to your Sparkol account and means we can credit your account.

We store this code when a user visits from a link you send out, so even if they revisit the site several times over several days and initially sign-up free, then uprade - you still get credited.

At the end of every month we pay out for the previous months payment through PayPal into your chosen PayPal account. You only need to specify this once you have had your first successful signup. There is no limit to the earnings or potential, but we do ask you to be clear, honest and moral in your communications and not to spam anyone. If we get reports of abuse or find that you are damaging the Sparkol brand, we will be left with no choice but to suspend your Affiliate involvement.
